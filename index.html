📖 Mr. Bunn's Widget Machine Guide

Convert communication symbol charts into beautifully timed karaoke animations and boards:

1. Upload Media Drag and drop your symbol chart PDF/images, and the song vocal/backing track. Set your song title!
2. Refine Grid Draw, delete, and resize the boxes around each symbol. You can also add your own image as a tile.
3. Sequence Path Click the symbol boxes in the exact order they should be read (1, 2, 3...) to establish the vocal timing order.
4. Sync & Export Play the audio and press the Spacebar/Enter or tap-button as each word is sung. Review and export!
💡 Keyboard Shortcuts: Spacebar/Enter to tap while recording. On the fine-tune timeline: ↑/↓ select a tile, ←/→ nudge its timing (hold Shift for bigger steps). Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y for Undo/Redo.
